Bio

I am an associate professor of Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology where I lead the Health and Happiness Design Lab. Previously, I held faculty positions at Drexel University's College of Computing and Informatics, University of California San Diego's School of Medicine, and Michigan State University's College of Communication Arts and Sciences. As PI, I have been awarded career grants as well as research grants from National Institutes of Health and National Science Foundation. I got a PhD from School of Information at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor. In my spare time, I play piano and tennis, learn Japanese, and consult my husband's local pediatric dentistry and orthodontics office.
